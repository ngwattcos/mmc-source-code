Some Competitions For High School Students
  • American Mathematics Competition 10/12 (AMC 10/12)
  • American Invitational Mathematics Examination (AIME) (qualification based on AMC 10/12)
  • USA Junior Mathematical Olympiad (USAJMO) (qualification based on AMC 10 and AIME)
  • USA Mathematical Olympiad (USAMO) (qualification based on AMC 12 and AIME)
  • International Mathematical Olympiad (IMO) (qualification based on elaborate selection process that begins with performance on USAJMO or USAMO the previous year)
  • Mandelbrot
  • New Jersey Mathematics League
  • Purple Comet! Math Meet
  • Interstellar Math Madness
  • American Regions Mathematics League (ARML)
  • Lehigh University Math Contest (Entrance Exam for Lehigh ARML team)
  • Princeton University Mathematics Competition (PUMAC)
  • Harvard MIT Math Tournament (HMMT)
  • Carnegie Mellon Informatics and Mathematics Competition (CMIMC)
  • Who Wants to Be a Mathematician
  • Math Prize for Girls
